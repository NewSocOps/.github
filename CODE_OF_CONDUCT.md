# Code of Conduct

We foster a respectful, inclusive environment for emerging developers globally.

## Expected
- Be welcoming and patient.
- Assume good intent, seek clarification.
- Provide constructive, specific feedback.
- Respect privacy and cultural differences.

## Unacceptable
- Harassment, discrimination, hate or derogatory speech.
- Doxxing or sharing private info.

## Reporting
Email (placeholder): team@newsocops.org  
If email is unavailable, open an Issue labeled `conduct-report` with minimal detail; maintainers will follow up privately.

## Enforcement
1. Warning
2. Temporary participation limits
3. Suspension
4. Immediate ban for severe violations

We aim for fairness and proportionality. Repeated violations may lead to permanent removal.
